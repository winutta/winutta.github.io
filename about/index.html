About Lablr

Lablr.org is a free platform housing animated tools, games, and experiences to engage with. These are aimed towards creating a relaxing visual and sound exploration into an idea, dataset, scene, or activity. With emphasis on interactibility user controlled action breathes movement into otherwise stagnant analysis, art, and simulations. This level of affecting what is on the screen in real time can allow useful and interesting new sides of existing things to be seen.
